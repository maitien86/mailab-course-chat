- **Midterm Exam (20%):** Closed-book eLearn quiz in Week 7 (Friday).
- **Final Exam (40%):** Comprehensive exam covering all topics; may include questions related to the **group project**.
- **Group Project (20%):** Runs from **Friday of Week 11 until Friday of Week 13**. 

# EXAM & PROJECT LOGISTICS
- **Secure Testing:** Both Midterm and Final exams **must** be completed using the **LockDown Browser**.
- **Group Formation:** The project is completed in **groups of 2 students**. Groups of 3 are permitted only if the total number of students in the section is odd.
- **Tools:** Students must ensure the Lockdown Browser is installed and working on their laptops before exams. A non-programmable calculator is required.

# COURSE ROADMAP
- **Weeks 1-4:** Algorithm Design and Analysis (Counting, Complexity, Iteration, Recursion).
- **Weeks 5-10:** Fundamental Data Structures (Stacks, Queues, Binary Trees, Heaps, Hash Maps, Graphs).
- **Weeks 11-13:** Computational Intractability & Heuristic Reasoning (Graph Algorithms, Limits of Computation).
